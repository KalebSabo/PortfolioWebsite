Dark Willow Games

Introduction:

Project 1 is a visual novel that my friends and I started a little over a year ago. This allowed me the opportunity to flex my muscles in Python Programming using the Ren'py game engine. I have posted much of the code that I created on my GitHub.

Game Progress:

The game is still in development, but we have made a lot of progress and are excited to see where it goes. Below you will find our tentative game title and logo that was created by our awesome artist "ghoolly"

Game Narrative:

The game is called GRASP, the setting is in a post apocalyptic city rife with crime and corruption by both sinister politicians and the supernatural. The player will explore the city then ultimately deciding the fate of the city and its inhabitants.

What are we doing right now?

We are currently working towards adding an adventure mode for the player to navigate through the city with pixel sprites and recreating the turn based combat system to be more engaging and fun for the player.

Unless otherwise stated, ALL artwork presented is by my good friend and artist, "ghoolly".
